Global environment puts pressure on payments system resilience

To strengthen the resilience of the payment system, it urgently needs to be possible to pay offline with cards and use cash to pay for essential goods. Both public and private payment system need to take action to increase payments system preparedness. The general public should have several payment options available in the event, or access to, a worst case scenario, according to the Riksbank's Payments Report presented today.

Are payments in Sweden efficient?

Payments in Sweden are, on the whole, efficient. Shops and service-related businesses accept different payment methods and find that payments work well, while the infrastructure is evolving to allow more payments to be instantaneous, but the private payment infrastructure for traditional batch payments needs to be upgraded. More and more initiatives are emerging to make cross-border payments smoother and cheaper, but there is still work to be done.
